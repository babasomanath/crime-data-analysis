Psychological drivers
Impunity normalisation
11% conviction rate signals mob justice is safe. Bandura: unpenalised crime is modelled by observers.
Identity scapegoating
Lynching victims "othered" (Bangladeshi, cow-killer). Economic anxiety displaces onto visible outgroups.
Digital mob formation
WhatsApp accelerates crowd mobilisation. Cuttack 2025 riots amplified by social media rumour spread.
Neighbouring states — border dynamics
West Bengal ↗ Migrant worker targeting; trafficking networks; communal spillover (Juyel Rana origin)
Jharkhand ↑ LWE corridor; mining crime; human trafficking into north Odisha
Chhattisgarh ← Maoist stronghold; Bastar–Malkangiri axis; weapon smuggling; 538 LWE crimes/yr
Andhra Pradesh ↓ Women trafficking; sand mafia; Ganjam border exploitation of migrant workers
